Super bikes

A super bike is a high performance motorcycle with a relatively higher engine capacity.
While there is no universal definition of a super bike, most motorcycles are called super bikes based on engine capacity and performance.
Typically, motorcycles in the 600cc engine capacity range and above qualify as super bikes. It is typical of most enthusiasts to term 1000cc motorbikes as super bikes
